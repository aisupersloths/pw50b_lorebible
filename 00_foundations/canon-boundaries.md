# Canon Boundaries

## Earth boundary

For practical narrative purposes, nobody knows Earth.

Do not introduce:
- remembered Earth place names
- remembered Solar System places
- remembered Earth religions or nations
- prestige lineages anchored to Earth
- recognizable terrestrial continuity myths

## Scope boundary

The setting contains both galaxy-wide truths and local world variation.
Do not elevate local examples into universal rules without explicit support.

## Example boundary

Illustrative percentages, sample worlds, and modeling heuristics are not automatically canon.
They become canon only if explicitly promoted.
