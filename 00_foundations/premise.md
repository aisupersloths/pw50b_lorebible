# Premise

## Summary

The setting takes place roughly 50 billion years in the future in the merged Milky Way–Andromeda galaxy.

## Canon

- Andromeda has merged with the Milky Way.
- Most stars are red dwarfs or otherwise very old stellar remnants.
- All other galaxies have retreated beyond the light cone.
- The descendants of Earth-origin life have colonized the galaxy multiple times over by sublight travel.
- Later civilizations developed wormhole networks spanning part of the galaxy, but far from all or even most stars are connected.
- Memory-sharing, copying, branching, and merging are foundational technologies on many connected worlds.
- Uploading and transcendence exist.
- Many transcended beings are inaccessible and incomprehensible to non-transcended minds.
- Technology level varies enormously by world.
- Archaeology and forgotten history are major sources of knowledge and innovation.
- Human-scale embodied minds persist not only because they remain locally useful in many niches, but also because person-derived agency remains a major productive substrate in many ordinary civilizations.

## Constraints

- The setting supports a wide range of local tech levels and genres.
- The intended initial focus is a high-tech localized setting with relatable viewpoint characters.
