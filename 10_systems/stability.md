# Stability

## Summary

Stability is a continuum across multiple axes, not a binary.

## Candidate axes

- demographic stability
- institutional stability
- economic stability
- ideological stability
- technological stability
- geopolitical stability
- identity stability

## Current working view

- Galaxy-wide regularities may be statistically stable over immense timescales.
- Local worlds may range from highly stable to highly unstable.
- A median world is not yet fixed in canon.
- It is plausible that many worlds maintain local equilibria lasting centuries.

## Important design consequence

The chosen stability level for a world will affect:
- class rigidity
- family strategy
- legal codification
- political conflict style
- archive politics
- story pacing
- whether change feels exceptional or routine
