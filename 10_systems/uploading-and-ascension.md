# Uploading and Ascension

## Canon

- Uploading is possible.
- Memories can interface with simulated brains.
- Backups and restores enable various forms of near-immortality.
- Uploads and biological minds are on a continuum rather than separated by a hard ontological wall.
- Ascension exists and can remove bodies from ordinary demographic circulation.
- Many transcended beings become inaccessible and incomprehensible to ordinary people.

## Constraints

- Not every story should foreground transcended beings.
- The existence of ascension changes demographics, inheritance, and social strategy even when transcended beings are offstage.
